📖 About This Library

Tahaffuz-E-Iman Library is not just another blog. It is a Digital Islamic Evidence Research Library designed to provide authentic answers to Islamic questions with proper references from:

  • ✅ Quran - Islamic primary source
  • ✅ Hadith - Prophet's traditions
  • ✅ Books - Islamic scholars' works
  • ✅ Fatwas - Authentic religious decrees
  • ✅ Scans & PDFs - Original evidence
  • ✅ Videos & Audio - Multimedia references

🎯 Our Mission

To create the world's largest evidence-based Islamic Q&A library where every answer is backed by authentic Islamic sources. We believe in providing knowledge with proof, not just opinions.
